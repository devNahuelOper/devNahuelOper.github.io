About me

Hóla world! I am a highly creative, driven and puzzle-loving software engineer, whose modus operandi is
How can I be better today, than I was yesterday?

One of my greatest joys in life is going from point a:nothing to point b:something...
Point A: A young boy, just emigrated from Argentina, total fish-out-of-water and so shy that he could barely look people in the eye.
Point B: That same boy, now a man, has trained and pursued a career as an actor for the better part of a decade, and can now express himself in front of hundreds of people.

The same pattern applies to my journey as a software engineer. In just sixteen weeks of App Academy’s rigorous, 80-100 hour a week program, I went from barely knowing what a for loop was, to being proficient in an array (pun intended) of languages such as Ruby, Ruby On Rails, Javascript, React/Redux, HTML5, CSS and a bunch more!

Additionally, I had three massive projects under my belt: 1) Full-Stack, which consists of cloning a site/app from the ground up (I chose Resident Advisor), 2) MERN group project, where I and three other programmers built a medical app dedicated to one’s vitals and 3) Javascript Project, where I built a musical game using nothing but Javascript, DOM Manipulation and Web Audio API.
